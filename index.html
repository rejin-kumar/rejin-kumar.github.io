About me

Since I was born I always had the affinity towards creativity. Don't know, think its in the blood. I've always loved sketching and painting. I then got digital with it by taking up Photoshop and became a self-learned digital-artist after I sort of jumped in. One more thing I believe I have is a natural curiosity to solve a problem in the most creative way possible. This brought out an analytical side of me I've never known existed in me before. I was introduced to the world of Web Design and Development in my academics during graduation. It's free to say my world changed after that. I started seeing the world in a whole new light (no I couldn't see the Matrix, yet!). I started researching on new design trends, learning new web technologies. The creative side of me started helping figuring out the best way to solve complex problems and build beautiful and aesthetically pleasing apps from scratch. I believe when you are in a field like this you need a creative and a calculative mind. This is the sole reason I felt The Web with its infinite possiblities was the place for me.
